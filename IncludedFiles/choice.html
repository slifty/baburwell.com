“a thought-provoking read more on the reverence for life than the right to die . . .”

The year is 2025. People live longer, but resources dwindle and growing numbers of elderly have become a burden – thus is spawned a government sponsored euthanasia project called Hollowdale. Matters of Choice follows the opening 3-week period and focuses on Levi, a young caseworker, who faces ethical, professional, and personal problems as he develops close relationships with his three assigned volunteers whose vibrant lives and spirits belie their choice to die.
